What are we doing?

The inspiration for this post is this beutiful vizualization from Mike Bostock. It nicely vizualizes the co-occurence of characters (when two characters appear in the same chapter) in the novel Les Mis√©rables by Victor Hugo using the data collected by Jacques Bertin (and his assistants).

The way this post will differentiate itself from this is that we are going to collect the data ourselves using named entity recognition. Named entity recognition is the dicipline of location and classifying named entities in text. Furthermore will we also try to cluster the characters according to their appearence in the novel.
