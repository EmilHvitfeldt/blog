Day 24: styler

Welcome back for the 24th and final day of the #packagecalendar, we are going to end in style with styler package by Lorenz Walthert and Kirill MÃ¼ller.

The package is available from CRAN and can be downloaded with

install.packages("styler")

The goal of styler is to provide non-invasive pretty-printing of R source code while adhering to the tidyverse formatting rules.

The package contains a handful of functions that helps you style your code at different levels. These functions include style_file(), style_dir() and style_pkg() who will style files, directories and whole packages respectively. Additionally styler provides Rstudio Addins for quick access.
